### Problem 1: Ladder Against the Wall  
A ladder is leaning against a wall. The bottom of the ladder is **6 m** away from the wall, and the ladder reaches a height of **8 m**.

**Solution:**  
$$[
c^2 = a^2 + b^2
]$$  
$$[
c^2 = 6^2 + 8^2
]$$  
$$[
c^2 = 36 + 64 = 100
]$$  
$$[

]$$  

âœ… The ladder is **10 m** long.
